Points to Remember

During Abhishek

  1. Please wear Dhoti Dupatta only after taking proper bath in wet towel.
  2. Do not touch any mat or cloth or any person who is not in dhoti dupatta.
  3. If wearing anything else under dhoti and dupatta please ensure it is properly cleaned as per guidelines in Jinvani
  4. Wear Dupatta properly so corners do not touch ground or any other item. It also looks more decent.
  5. Do not wear any Karha, Moli, Band, Rakhi, wrist watch as they are not properly cleaned. We wear them all day and they must be removed before touching the idol.
  6. Apply Chandan on Forehead, both upper arms, wrist, above navel, chest and upper-back.
  7. Wash hands properly before touching the idol.
  8. Be patient and calm, all are given equal oppurtunity for abhishek and prakshal.
  9. If you see anyone not following proper procedure, feel free to guide them properly so as to maintain the purity as per terahpanthi amnae.
  10. If someone suggests anything please do not take it as a remark rather a positive feedback, we all have to succeed as a big family
  11. Please take Gand-udhak only after washing hands and with spoon kept there in for this purpose.
  12. Idols for Abhishek on each day have been identified in a linear order, please do not enforce for doing Abhishek on any idol based upon day, all days are of all Teerthankaras.
  13. After prakshal please ensure to wash Dhoti Dupatta and spread on rope properly to dry.

In Jinalay

  1. Do not wear/carry anything made of leather inside the Jinalay like Slippers, Shoes, Wallet, Belt, wrist watch, jeans with leather tag, etc.
  2. During Abhishek please take care of children, so they do not touch anyone in dhoti dupatta.
  3. Strictly follow Digamber Jain Terahpanthi Amnae, do not offer any fruits or flowers or any other sachet dravya.
  4. Please take Gand-udhak only after washing hands and with spoon kept there in for this purpose.
  5. Do not litter water or dravya or Gand-udhak on floor or tables. Please maintain cleanliness and do not disrespect anything.
  6. Ensure to wash hands and feet before entering the premises.
  7. Kindly keep the Jinvani and Shahstaji back from where you took it.
  8. Upon completion of poojan please place back poojan thali at designated place do not leave it on table.
  9. Perform poojan in low voice so it does not disturb others.
  10. Apart from Abhishek and Prakshal timings and those who are in proper dhoti dupatta nobody is allowed to touch idols (charansparsh).
  11. Please contribute timely towards monthly fund for properly maintenance of Jinalay.
  12. Take care of opening and closing time of Jinalay.
  13. Everyone here is a family, please do not get hurt or hurt anyone intentionally.
  14. Kindly drive slowly within the Vishali Nagar society limit and do not argue with any of the society members.
  15. Do not park vehicle just outside the gate of Jinalay.
  16. Try to use 2-wheeler instead of 4-wheeler so no one in society complains about parking as the front area used for parking is not our property.
  17. Ensure to collect receipt of any donation (daan) made towards Jinalay.
