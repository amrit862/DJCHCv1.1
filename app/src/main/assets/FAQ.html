Frequently Asked Questions



Ques. Do we have a Digamber Jain Temple near Hitex, Gachibowli, Miyapur?
Ans. Yes, we have a Digamber Jain Temple near Hitex, Gachibowli and Miyapur.

Ques. Which is the nearest Digamber Jain temple in Hitex, Gachibowli, Miyapur
Ans. Shree 1008 Chandraprabh Sarovar Jinalay (DJCHC - Digamber Jain Center of Hitech City) is nearest. For exact location and other Jain temples in Hyderabad, visit Hyderabad Temples section of the app.

Ques. How did the Jinalay came into existence and what all problems were faced initially?
Ans. For all these details please refer About Us section of the app.

Ques. We want to take boli for a religiuos proceeding at Jinalay, whom should we contact?
Ans. It has been a practice in DJCHC of not having any boli for any religious proceeding so far even in construction, Vedi Pratishtha and everything. We are committed to abide by this practice forever.
For any religious proceeding a fixed amount(Nyochawar Rashee) is decided and announced in advance.

Ques. What are the opening and closing timings of Sarovar Jinalay?
Ans. Although temple should ideally remain open across the day, however for security reasons below timings have been identified:
Morning: 6.30 am to 11.30am
Evening: 6.00 pm to 8.30 pm

Ques. What if I happen to be late or early on a given day, shall I return without having oppurtunity to have Dev Darshan?
Ans. No, not at all. You can call Rakesh Panditji, who resides in Jinalay itself and he will open it up for you. For contact number please refer Contact Us section of the app.
Please note, that this method should not be made a regular practice.

Ques. Which customs are followed in Jinalay for Dev Darshan, Pooja, Abhishek, etc?.
Ans. We strictly follow Digamber Jain Terahpanthi Amnae. Sachet dravya (like Fruits, Flowers, etc.) chadhana purnatya nishedh hai.
We request full coordination from all our members on this to keep things and working smooth.

Ques. What are the timings of Abhishek at Sarovar Jinalay?
Ans.Abhishek starts strictly as per below timings unless otherwise notified:
Weekday: 7am
Weekend: 8am
Note: Abhishek is followed by Shanti Dhara daily. Daily Abhishek is performed on three idols and on idols on Sunday. Abhishek is done once only.

Ques. I have never done Abhishek before and I am unaware of the process, I want to start doing Abhishek whom should I contact?
Ans. We, the DJCHC family encourage more and more people, youngsters, kids (above the age of 8 years) to start doing the Abhishek. You just need to be on time on any given day and any of us will guide you, right from wearing dhoti till the end of entire process.

Ques. We wish to do a vidhan in Jinalay, what is the process for that?
Ans. For doing Vidhaan, please contact Rakesh Panditji. There will be some fixed amount for doing vidhaan, as it involves usage of Samgri and another utilities of Jinalay. Proper receipt would be given for same. Any other amount over and above fixed amount as donation is always welcomed.

Ques. We wish to do some religious anushthan at home but we are unaware of the process, what should we do?
Ans. Please contact Rakesh Panditji. If he is able to assist you with the same or otherwise he will guide you to proper person. Any commitment about donation or payment will be based on your discussion with Rakesh Panditji, Jinalay authority (DJCHC) will not interfere in this unless it is impacting daily work for Jinalay.

Ques. I want to take Granth/Shashtraji for swadhyay to my home, what should I do?
Ans. We have kept a register in Jinalay, please make an entry into the register and inform Rakesh Panditji for the same. Below points must be kept in mind for same:
1. These are religious documents and proper respect should be given to them at home like keeping in safe place, not keeping on lap or ground, not touching with dirty hands, etc.
2. Only if three or more copies of same document are available in Jinalay then it can be taken, otherwise one must do Swadhyay in Jinalay itself.
3. Please ensure to return the document within seven days of taking it.
4. Please ensure no pages or cover are torn.
